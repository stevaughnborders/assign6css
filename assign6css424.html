Chicago Bulls Derrick Rose MVP

Derrick Rose A kid from chicago was the youngest MVP in NBA history has retired.In the 2008 draft, the Chicago Bulls selected him as the first overall pick. He led the National Team to play in the Olympics in Beijing. He missed the game for 3 months due to tendinitis in his right knee. He returned to the court in October and played all eight preseason games. After LeBron James, he was the first player to win the Rookie of the Year award. He scored 16.8 points, 6.3 assists, and 3.9 rebounds in each game.
